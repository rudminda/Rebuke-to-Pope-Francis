the Rebuke:

This rebuke has been years-in-the-making, mainly from listening to Church Militant, the Lepanto Institute, and LifeSiteNews.com, for so many years, which I generally enthusiastically endorse. I am allowed to make this rebuke, on the basis of Canon 212, which says that I can make known to the pastors and other Christians my thoughts about the good of the church, while preserving reverence for the pastors, and their dignity. I feel like I have largely done that in this rebuke.

Beware that the original English version is the only version whose . . .

the author:
